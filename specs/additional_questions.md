The "Pulse Check" Question Set

Ask user one of the questions that he did not answer yet and update profile.

1. The "Sunday Morning" Test

"If you had a free Sunday in a city you already know and love, would you head straight to your 'old reliable' favorite café, or go find a neighborhood you’ve never stepped foot in?"

The Insight: Measures Loyalty vs. Novelty. This tells the bot whether to suggest "hidden gems" or "reliable classics" for the rest of the trip.

2. The "Suitcase Quirk"

"What’s the most unusual or 'non-essential' thing you always manage to find room for in your suitcase?"

The Insight: Reveals Identity & Comfort. Whether it’s a specific brand of tea, a silk pillowcase, or a heavy camera lens, it tells the bot what makes the user feel "at home" anywhere.

3. The "Unpopular Opinion"

"Give me one 'unpopular opinion' about a famous travel destination. What’s a place everyone loves that just didn't click for you?"

The Insight: Identifies Authenticity Triggers. This helps the bot filter out "hyped" spots that might not align with the user’s actual taste.

4. The "Research vs. Reflex"

"Are you the type to have 20 tabs open with research before you land, or do you prefer to just ask a local for a recommendation once you're there?"

The Insight: Refines Information Sourcing. It tells the bot if it should provide "Data-Heavy" guides or "Community-Driven" tips.

5. The "Teleport" Prompt

"If I could teleport you to any landscape right now—a jagged mountain peak, a white-sand beach, or a neon-lit city street—where are we going?"

The Insight: Captures Immediate Aesthetic Craving. This is a great "transitory trait" question to ask when planning a new weekend getaway.

6. The "Photo Reel"

"When you’re scrolling back through your photos after a trip, what do you usually have the most of: food, architecture, nature, or candid shots of people?"

The Insight: Defines Primary Value. It tells the bot if the user is a "Visualist," a "Foodie," or a "Socialite."

7. The "Dinner Vibe"

"On a scale of 'Total Silence' to 'Lively Jazz Club,' what’s the background noise of your ideal dinner tonight?"

The Insight: Maps Environmental Sensitivity. This helps the bot pick restaurant or hotel environments that won't overstimulate (or underwhelm) the user.

8. The "Guilty Pleasure"

"What’s your go-to 'guilty pleasure' when you’re on the road? (e.g., watching local TV in the hotel, finding a McDonald’s in a foreign country, or sleeping in until noon?)"

The Insight: Identifies Stress-Relief Patterns. This tells the bot when the user might need a "break" from the intensity of travel.

9. The "Menu Gamble"

"When you see something on a menu that you don't recognize at all, is your first instinct to order it just to see what it is, or do you play it safe with something you know you'll like?"

The Insight: Measures Micro-Risk Tolerance. This is the best way to gauge how "daring" the bot’s next suggestion should be.

10. The "Artifact" Question

"What’s the one thing you’ve brought back from a trip that still sits on your desk or shelf today?"

The Insight: Reveals Long-term Value. It tells the bot whether the user values physical craftsmanship, intellectual stories, or simple nostalgia.
