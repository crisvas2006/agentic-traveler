# Tally Onboarding Form — "Know Thy Self (for Travel)"

**URL:** https://tally.so/r/9qN6p4

**Purpose:** Collects traveler preferences and personality traits to build
a user profile for the Agentic Traveler companion bot.

**Intro text:** _"This form helps in building your profile so your travel
companion will be able to help you! Don't worry if your preferences change
over time, you can always redo this questionnaire and it will overwrite
the previous one."_

  - C: Comfort-first traveler — I want nice stays, good food, and minimal stress, even if it costs
  - D: Big spender when inspired — If the vibe's right, I'll drop cash like a drunk celeb
  - E: Varies a lot — depends on the trip, timing, or mood swings
